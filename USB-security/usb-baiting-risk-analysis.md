## USB Baiting Risk Assessment – Parking Lot Scenario

Analyzed a potential USB baiting attack scenario involving a hospital employee’s flash drive. Evaluated the risks from both attacker and defender perspectives and identified mitigation strategies.

**Key Risks Identified:**
- Exposure of personally identifiable information (PII)
- Social engineering targeting HR personnel
- Malware delivery through USB baiting
- Potential network backdoor establishment

**Controls Recommended:**
- Disable AutoRun and restrict USB ports
- Implement endpoint detection and response (EDR)
- Enforce antivirus and system monitoring
- Conduct employee security awareness training
