# Incident Handler Journal – Entry #1

## Scenario
A small U.S. healthcare clinic experienced a ransomware attack that encrypted critical patient records and disrupted business operations.

## Incident Type
Ransomware attack delivered through targeted phishing emails containing malicious attachments.

## 5 W’s Analysis

**Who:**  
An organized group of unethical hackers targeting healthcare organizations.

**What:**  
Ransomware encrypted critical files and systems, preventing access to patient records.

**When:**  
Tuesday at approximately 9:00 a.m.

**Where:**  
Internal network and computer systems of a small U.S. healthcare clinic.

**Why:**  
Attackers gained access through phishing emails with malicious attachments, then deployed ransomware for financial gain.

## Key Observations
- Lack of phishing awareness training may have contributed.
- Email filtering controls could reduce future risk.
- Secure backups are critical in ransomware response.
- A formal incident response plan is essential.

## Skills Demonstrated
- Incident documentation
- 5 W’s structured analysis
- Ransomware investigation reasoning
- Initial response evaluation
